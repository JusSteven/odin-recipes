Beef Lentil Stew

Decription

Beef lentil stew is a South Morrocan dish originating from the African mainland.

Ingredients

  • 1kg of beef steak
  • 2 tablespoon of oil or more if needed
  • 4 carrots
  • 1 onion
  • 4 cups of beef broth
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 cup of dry lentils
  • 11 potatoes
  • Ground peppper
  • Garlic
  • Coriander leaves

Meal Prep

  1. Dice the onions
  2. Dice the carrots
  3. Slice the tomatoes into small bits
  4. Grate the garlic on the sliced tomato
  5. Pluck the corainader leaves
  6. Wash the chicken

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over high heat.
  2. Add steak cubes and sear all sides till browned.
  3. Transfer the cubes to a plate aside
  4. Add the carrots and garlic to the same skillet cook until softened.
  5. Add beef broth with tomatoes with juice, the lentils browned steak cubes and stir.
  6. Reduce the heat cover the skillet halfway with a lid and simmmer for 40 mins.
  7. Add potatoes simmer until the lentils and potatoes are sof.
  8. Season with salt, pepper and coriander
