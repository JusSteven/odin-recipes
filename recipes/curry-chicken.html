Curry Chicken Stew

Decription

Curry chicken is a South Asian dish originating from the Indian subcontinent.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 700g of chicken
  • Garlic
  • coriander
  • vegatable oil
  • A jug of water

Meal Prep

  1. Dice the onions
  2. Dice the bell peppers
  3. Slice the tomatoes into small bits
  4. Grate the garlic on the sliced tomato
  5. Pluck the corainader leaves
  6. Wash the chicken

Steps

  1. Put the onions and bell peppers into the pot.
  2. Add 10ml of vegetable oil.
  3. Stir continously until the contents are tender.
  4. Add the washed chicken with half a cup of water.
  5. Add a table spoon of salt and curry powder.
  6. Let the contents boil for 10 minutes, stir after every two minutes.
  7. Add the sliced tomatoes and grated garlic to the broth.
  8. Wait for 6 minutes.
  9. Stir the chicken making sure the tomatoes are broken down.
  10. After making sure the tomatoes are broken down taste for spicing add accordingly and turn off the heat
  11. Add the coriander, stir a bit then close the lid
  12. Let it sit for 3 minutes then serve with rice
